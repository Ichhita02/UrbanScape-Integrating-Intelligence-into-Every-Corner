Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

UrbanScape is a smart home automation system designed to enhance the comfort, security, and convenience of your living space. It integrates multiple IoT-enabled features like automated switchboards, plant watering, and door security systems.

The automated switchboard system allows you to control your home appliances remotely, making it easier to manage lights, fans, and other electrical devices. You can use the mobile app to turn devices on or off based on your preferences.

The plant watering system ensures your plants receive the right amount of water at scheduled times. Based on environmental conditions, the system automatically waters the plants without the need for manual intervention, ensuring their optimal growth.
